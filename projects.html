Design of a mind controlled hand prosthesis using Machine Learning

This project combines various technologies and components to create a complete hand prosthesis. It integrates the mechanical Cyborg Beast hand with a CAD-designed forearm, making it accessible for home development using Arduino components. The hand's movements are controlled by tensor cables attached to step motors. Python is used for software development and data analysis, while OpenVibe is employed for processing EEG signals. EEG signals, captured from the user's brain activity, are utilized to develop a brain-computer interface. The LDA machine learning algorithm achieves high accuracy with 84.7% in interpreting the user's intentions based on the EEG data. .

Smart Shower

Introducing our revolutionary smart shower system, designed to revolutionize your bathing experience while promoting water conservation. With cutting-edge technology, including electrovalves, temperature sensors, and ultrasonic sensors, we have created a shower that not only enhances your comfort but also saves a remarkable 60 liters of water per bath. Our smart shower is incredibly versatile, offering two installation options. For existing homes, we can seamlessly integrate our system into your current shower setup, instantly upgrading it to an eco-friendly solution. Alternatively, if you're building a new home or undergoing renovations, our smart shower can be installed as a brand-new system, providing you with the ultimate bathing experience from the start. Now, let's delve deeper into the ingenious features of our smart shower. The electrovalves integrated into our system ensure precise control over water flow, allowing you to customize your shower experience according to your preferences. The temperature sensors guarantee a comfortable bathing temperature throughout your shower, eliminating sudden bursts of cold or hot water. But what truly sets our smart shower apart is the utilization of ultrasonic sensors. These sensors enable our system to detect when the shower water is being wasted or not actively utilized by the user. Instead of letting this water go down the drain, our technology enables its redirection for other purposes within your home. This innovative approach ensures that not a single drop of water is wasted, as every ounce is efficiently repurposed. Imagine the positive impact this could have on your household's water consumption. With our smart shower, you'll not only enjoy a luxurious and tailored shower experience but also contribute to conserving this precious resource. Clicl on the image to view video demo.

Chrome Extension for Linkedin

I am developing a Chrome Extension for the company Wow Work that utilizes OAuth to connect to Google's Spreadsheet API. The extension allows users to gather public data of their choice from LinkedIn. It dynamically modifies the DOM content of LinkedIn pages and injects custom scripts as needed. The Chrome Extension offers a seamless and user-friendly experience for gathering public data from LinkedIn while leveraging Google's Spreadsheet API for efficient storage and management. It provides a convenient solution for users who need to extract and organize LinkedIn data for various purposes.
